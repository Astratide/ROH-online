Arknights Classes

In Arknights, players must deploy Operators of different classes strategically to fend off enemy waves. In this supplement, each class is just as vital in combat.

The eight classes in the game are Vanguard, Guard, Defender, Sniper, Caster, Medic, Supporter, and Specialist. Below is a brief introduction to each class and their page references in this supplement:


Vanguard

Overview: Vanguards are the forward vanguard of the squad, responsible for quick deployment and DP generation at the start of battle.

Role: Similar to the D&D Fighter, Ranger, or Rogue, Vanguards charge into battle and provide early combat advantages for their allies. They also use their skills outside of combat to solve problems for the team.

Hit Die: D8

Proficiencies: Light armor, medium armor, simple/martial weapons

Key Ability: Strength or Dexterity


Guard

Overview: Guards are the mainstay of melee damage output, capable of dealing heavy damage to enemies on the front line.

Role: Similar to the D&D Fighter or Monk, Guards are typically high-damage ground-based physical damage dealers, also possessing some protective or utility functions.

Hit Die: D10

Proficiencies: Light armor, medium armor, heavy armor, shields, simple weapons, martial melee weapons

Key Ability: Strength or Dexterity


Defender

Overview: Defenders are the shield of the squad, responsible for absorbing enemy damage and protecting allies.

Role: Similar to the D&D Paladin or Barbarian, Defenders are characterized by high defense and survivability. They can heal or protect allies on the battlefield, and sometimes deal respectable damage as well.

Hit Die: D12

Proficiencies: Light armor, medium armor, heavy armor, shields, simple weapons, martial melee weapons

Key Ability: Constitution


Sniper

Overview: Snipers are the mainstay of ranged physical damage output, capable of delivering lethal strikes from a distance.

Role: Similar to the D&D Fighter or Ranger, Snipers excel at ranged physical attacks and precision strikes.

Hit Die: D8

Proficiencies: Light armor, simple weapons, martial ranged weapons

Key Ability: Dexterity


Caster

Overview: Casters are the mainstay of ranged magical damage output, capable of dealing spell damage to enemies.

Role: Similar to the D&D Wizard, Warlock, or Sorcerer, Casters specialize in powerful magic attacks that can pierce heavily armored enemies.

Hit Die: D6

Proficiencies: Simple weapons

Key Ability: Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma


Medic

Overview: Medics are the healers of the squad, responsible for restoring allies' HP.

Role: Similar to the D&D Cleric or Druid, Medics focus on healing, support, and assisting allies. Some Medics also have some offensive capability.

Hit Die: D8

Proficiencies: Light armor, medium armor, simple weapons

Key Ability: Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma


Supporter

Overview: Supporters are the buffers of the squad, providing various enhancement effects or weakening enemies.

Role: Similar to the D&D Bard, Wizard, or Artificer, Supporters are characterized by providing buffs and control for their allies.

Hit Die: D8

Proficiencies: Light armor, medium armor, simple weapons

Key Ability: Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma


Specialist

Overview: Specialists are the all-rounders of the squad, capable of performing various special tasks such as displacement, control, assassination, etc.

Role: Similar to the D&D Rogue or Ranger, Specialists are characterized by flexible and versatile tactics.

Hit Die: D8

Proficiencies: Light armor, medium armor, shields, simple weapons, martial melee weapons

Key Ability: Intelligence or Dexterity
