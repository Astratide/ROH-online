Patriot

Melee Physical; Sarkaz Leader (Wendigo)

Patriot. Buldrokkas'tee, the "Patriot." His armor, long unmaintained, is scarred in many places. His once-sharp halberd has been worn dull by rust and erosion. But Patriot never wavers, never retreats, never shows mercy. He has wrestled with fate for so long, and he will cut open fate's throat.


    "Rhodes Island, I march."


Patriot

Large Humanoid (Sarkaz; Wendigo), Lawful Neutral

AC 24 Initiative +18 (28)
HP 500 (40d10+280)
Speed 40 ft.
ModSave ModSave ModSave
STR 26 +8 +16 DEX 15 +2 +2 CON 24 +7 +15
INT 16 +3 +3 WIS 18 +4 +12 CHA 18 +4 +4
Skills Athletics +16, Perception +12, Intimidation +12, History +11, Religion +11
Resistances Poison, Thunder, Cold
Immunities Necrotic; Blinded, Deafened, Charmed, Frightened, Poisoned
Senses Blindsight 60 ft., Darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 22
Languages Common, Ursine, Sarkaz
CR 25 (75,000 XP, or Mythic 150,000; PB +8)

Traits

Boss Resistance (3/Day). When Patriot fails a saving throw, he can expend 50 HP to instead succeed. Additionally, Patriot can take Legendary Action and expend a use of Boss Resistance to regain 50 HP.

Magic Resistance. Patriot has Advantage on saving throws made to resist spells or other magical effects.

Heavy Fortress. Patriot treats any critical hit against him as a normal hit. Additionally, all piercing, slashing, and bludgeoning damage Patriot takes has a -8 penalty. Finally, when resisting effects that cause the Prone condition or forced movement, Patriot's size is treated as one size larger, and he has Advantage on the saving throw.

Destruction Stance (Mythic Trait; Recharges on Long Rest). While Patriot's HP is reduced to 0, he doesn't die. At the start of his next turn, he enters Destruction Stance, his HP resets to 500, his AC resets to 18, he finishes recharging Meteorite Strike, and he regains all expended uses of Boss Resistance. Additionally, for one hour, Patriot can use the options in the "Mystic Actions" section.

Life Sacrifice (Active only during Destruction Stance). Each time Patriot ends his turn, he deals 10 necrotic damage to each enemy within 10 ft. of his Emanation.


Actions

Multiattack (March Stance only). Patriot makes four Halberd attacks. He can replace one of these attacks with Spearshot.

Halberd. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 10 ft. Hit: 19 (2d10+8) slashing damage, and the target or another creature within 5 ft. of it takes 11 (2d10) thunder damage. Miss: 11 (2d10) thunder damage.

Spearshot. Ranged Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, range 120 ft. Hit: 41 (6d10+8) piercing damage, and the target's speed is reduced by 10 ft. until the end of its next turn. Hit or Miss: The weapon immediately returns to Patriot after being used for a ranged attack.

Meteorite Strike (Recharge 5-6). Patriot immediately makes two Halberd attacks, then hurls his spear into the sky. Dexterity Saving Throw: DC 22, one visible creature within 300 ft. Failure: 63 (10d10+8) piercing damage plus 22 (4d10) thunder damage, and Prone. Success: Half damage only. Hit or Miss: The weapon immediately returns to Patriot after being used for a ranged attack.


Bonus Actions

Guerrilla Aegis (3/Day). Patriot chooses himself and each ally within 120 ft. that can hear or see him, bolstering their morale and granting each target 50 HP of Temporary HP.


Reactions

Tactical Command. Trigger: An ally who can hear or see Patriot misses with an attack roll. Response: If Patriot can see the ally, the ally immediately rerolls the attack with Advantage. If it still misses, Patriot regains his Reaction at the end of this turn.


Legendary Actions

Legendary Actions: 3. Patriot can take one of the following actions immediately after another creature's turn. He regains all Legendary Actions at the start of his turn.

Swift Strike. Patriot immediately makes one Halberd attack.

Battle March. Each ally within 30 ft. of Patriot can use its Reaction to move up to half its speed and make one attack, but can't attack the same target. At the same time, Patriot can also move up to half his speed.


Mystic Actions

If Patriot's Destruction Stance trait is activated, he can choose from the following options as Legendary Actions.

Meteor Returns. Patriot immediately finishes recharging Meteorite Strike.

Destructive Assault. Patriot immediately moves up to half his speed toward one visible enemy, then makes one Halberd or Spearshot attack.
