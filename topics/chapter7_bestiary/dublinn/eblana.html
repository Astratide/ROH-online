Leader of the Dublinn

Melee, Ranged, Physical, Art; Humanoid Leader (Draco)

The Leader of the Dublinn. A Draco whose fire consumes life. A terrifying shadow. Do not let her corner you.

Necromantic Fireseed. Death chooses its targets with irrational caprice. But once chosen, it becomes relentless, watching its quarry until the very end of their days.


    "My body is cold, my senses dulled. Within me churns an endless cold that burns like my own flame... yet that very pain is what fascinates me. Without it, I might forget that I have tamed death."


Necrass

Medium Humanoid (Dragon; Draco), Lawful Neutral

AC 18 (Mage Armor) Initiative +12 (22)
HP 280 (33d8+132)
Speed 35 ft.
ModSave ModSave ModSave
STR 18 +4 +4 DEX 21 +5 +5 CON 18 +4 +11
INT 16 +3 +3 WIS 16 +3 +10 CHA 25 +7 +7
Skills Arcana +10, History +10, Perception +10, Insight +10, Deception +13, Intimidation +13
Resistances Fire, Necrotic, Poison
Immunities Blinded, Deafened, Poisoned, Charmed
Senses Darkvision 120 ft., Blindsight 30 ft., passive Perception 20
Languages Common, Victorian, Terra
CR 22 (41,000 XP, or Mythic 82,000; PB +7)

Traits

Necromantic Fireseed. When Necrass causes a creature starts Burning, this burning deals 7 (1d12) necrotic damage per instance instead of normal fire damage, and the creature is also affected by Burn Mark. In addition, when Necrass reduces a creature's HP to 0, that creature gains one level of Exhaustion, and Necrass regains 26 (4d12) HP.

Magic Resistance. Necrass has Advantage on saving throws made to resist spells or other magical effects.

Boss Resistance (3/Day). When Necrass fails a saving throw, she can expend 50 HP to instead succeed. Additionally, Necrass can take Legendary Action and expend a use of Boss Resistance to regain 50 HP.

The Reignition (Mythic Trait; Recharges on Long Rest). While Necrass's HP is reduced to 0, she doesn't die. At the start of her next turn, her HP resets to 280 and she regains all expended uses of Boss Resistance. Additionally, for one hour, Necrass can use the options in the "Mystic Actions" section.


Actions

Multiattack. Necrass makes two attacks using either Necrotic Flame or Wreckal Halberd. She can replace one of these attacks with a Artcasting action.

Necrotic Flame. Ranged Attack Roll: +14, range 120 ft. Hit: 18 (4d8) necrotic damage. If the target is Bloodied or Burning, it deals an additional 9 (2d8) psychic damage.

Wreckal Halberd. Melee Attack Roll: +13, reach 10 ft. Hit: 22 (3d10+6) slashing damage, and the target starts Burning .

The Usurpation of Life (1/Day). Necrass kills any number of willing allies within 60 ft., gaining a number of Temporary HP equal to their combined maximum HP (caps at 200).

Artcasting. Necrass casts one of the following spells as a 14th-level caster, using Charisma as her spellcasting ability (Spell Save DC 22, Spell Attack +14).

At will: Toll the Dead, Prestidigitation, Minor Illusion, Mage Hand, Mage Armor (cast before combat)

3/day each: Bane, Hold Person, Bestow Curse, Revivify

2/day each: Blight (5th-level version)

1/day each: Circle of Death, Animate Dead (7th-level version; cast before combat if possible)


Bonus Actions

Scorched Domination. Constitution Saving Throw: DC 22, one creature Burning within 60 ft. of Necrass. Failure: 22 (4d10) necrotic damage, and the target is Incapacitated until the end of its next turn.

Crown the Dead (1/Day). Necrass chooses one visible ally within 60 ft. and transfers up to 100 HP of Temporary HP from herself to that creature. While this Temporary HP persists, the target's AC can't be less than 20, and each time it makes an attack it deals an additional 9 (2d8) necrotic damage, or 13 (2d12) necrotic damage if the target is Bloodied.


Reactions

Resurrection. Trigger: A creature Necrass can see within 60 ft. dies. Response: Necrass summons a Flamechaser Elite Soldier with 0 HP at its original location. These summoned creatures are Necrass's allies, have Initiative 10, and disappear after 1 hour.


Legendary Actions

Legendary Actions: 3 (4 in lair). Necrass can take one of the following actions immediately after another creature's turn. She regains all Legendary Actions at the start of her turn.

Death Bell. Necrass uses Artcasting to cast Toll the Dead.

Pinching Off. Necrass chooses any number of Burning creatures she can see within 120 ft., dealing 13 (2d12) necrotic damage to each.

Swift Artcasting. Necrass uses her Artcasting feature to cast a spell of 3rd level or lower with a casting time of 1 action. She can't do so again until the start of her next turn.


Mystic Actions

If Necrass's The Reignition trait is activated, she can choose from the following options as Legendary Actions.

Wither Engagement. Necrass immediately makes one Necrotic Flame or Wreckal Halberd attack.

Necromancy Wildfire (1/Day). Wisdom Saving Throw: DC 21, each enemy within 60 ft. of her Emanation. Failure: 14 (4d6) psychic damage, and the target gains the Burning condition and is Frightened while Burning. Failure or Success: The ground in the area is set ablaze for 1 minute. Any creature Necrass chooses that enters the area for the first time on a turn, or starts its turn there, takes 7 (1d12) necrotic damage.
