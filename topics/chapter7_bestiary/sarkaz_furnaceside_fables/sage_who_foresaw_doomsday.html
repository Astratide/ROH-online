Rhodes Operation Handbook：[Sage Who Foresaw Doomsday]
The Doomsday Prophet

Melee Physical; Feline Leader

Commander of the Anti-Kazdel Coalition. The Mediator has made a choice that brooks no hesitation. The time to face the "Six Heroes" is far more urgent than anticipated. She will not allow herself to fail, nor will she yield. She will choose to be a thoroughgoing adversary.


Kalt'ist

Medium Humanoid (Feline), Lawful Neutral

AC 17 (Mage Armor) Initiative +14 (24)
HP 225 (30d8+90)
Speed 35 ft.
ModSave ModSave ModSave
STR 11 +0 +6 DEX 18 +4 +10 CON 15 +2 +2
INT 22 +6 +6 WIS 19 +4 +10 CHA 17 +3 +3
Skills Arcana +12, History +18, Investigation +12, Religion +12, Insight +9, Persuasion +9
Immunities Psychic; Frightened, Charmed (Mind Blank)
Senses Darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 18
Languages All Terran Languages
CR 17 (18,000 XP; PB +6)

Traits

Magic Resistance. Kalt'ist has Advantage on saving throws made to resist spells or other magical effects.

Synchronized Resistance (3/Day). When Kalt'ist fails a saving throw, she can change the result to a success. If Mon2tr is within 60 ft. of Kalt'ist and she fails a saving throw, she can also expend a use of Synchronized Resistance to change the result to a success.

Feline's Agility. Kalt'ist has a +1d4 bonus on Dexterity saving throws and Dexterity ability checks.

Phase Anchor. Kalt'ist is immune to forced movement and teleportation effects at her own discretion.


Actions

Multiattack. Kalt'ist makes two Blade of Disaster attacks. She can replace one of these attacks with casting a spell with a casting time of 1 action.

Blade of Disaster. Melee Attack Roll: +12 (has Advantage while under Foresight), reach 5 ft. Hit: 26 (4d12) force damage. This attack scores a critical hit on a roll of 18 or higher on the d20, dealing 78 (12d12) force damage instead.

Artcasting. Kalt'ist casts one of the following spells as a 17th-level caster, using Intelligence as her spellcasting ability (Spell Save DC 20, Spell Attack +12).

At will:Ray of Frost, Mage Hand, Prestidigitation, Mage Armor* (included in AC)

3/day each:Hold Person, Dispel Magic, Slow, Identify

2/day each:Confusion, Legend Lore, Steel Wind Strike

1/day each:Disintegrate, Command, Mind Blank*, Foresight*

*Kalt'ist casts these marked spells on herself before combat.


Bonus Actions

Summon Mon2tr (3/Day). Kalt'ist summons Mon2tr at a visible unoccupied space within 30 ft. to assist her in combat. Mon2tr has its own separate Initiative in combat and is an Ally of Kalt'ist. If Mon2tr already exists, she instead restores 11 (2d10) HP to both herself and Mon2tr.


Reactions

Transposition (Recharge 5-6). Trigger: Kalt'ist is about to be hit by an attack. Response: Kalt'ist can swap positions with Mon2tr, changing the target of the attack to Mon2tr, while causing a spatial shockwave — Dexterity Saving Throw: DC 20, each creature within 10 ft. of either Kalt'ist or Mon2tr. Failure: 26 (4d12) force damage. Success: Half damage.

Protective Magic (3/Day). Kalt'ist casts Counterspell or Shield (see those spells for trigger conditions), using Intelligence as her spellcasting ability.


Legendary Actions

Legendary Actions: 3 (4 in lair). Kalt'ist can take one of the following actions immediately after another creature's turn. She regains all Legendary Actions at the start of her turn.

Command: Structural Fortification. If Mon2tr is within 60 ft. of Kalt'ist, she grants both herself and Mon2tr 11 (2d10) HP of Temporary HP.

Command: Tactical Coordination. If Mon2tr is within 60 ft. of Kalt'ist, she commands Mon2tr to immediately make two attacks.

Command: Meltdown. If Mon2tr is within 60 ft. of Kalt'ist, she commands Mon2tr to immediately make two attacks. If the attacks hit, each deals an additional 14 force damage that can't be reduced. If the attacks miss, Mon2tr loses 14 HP for each miss.
