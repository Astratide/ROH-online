Theresa, Black-crowned Loremistress

Ranged Art; Sarkaz Leader

Theresa, Black-crowned Loremistress. The weaver of dreams, yet her gaze does not linger long upon those pure-white petals. The true responsibility bestowed upon her and her brother has yet to arrive — the weight of the crown reminds them of this at all times.

The Babel Oath. Thus we behold — Therasia, and Theresis... they crown each other. The Sarkaz thus enter an era of dual monarchy. Some celebrate, celebrating the dawn of this new age; some fear, fearing this unprecedented anomaly in history. What lies ahead, we cannot yet know.


    "A humble hero, a magnificent dream. The future condenses in her eyes, and the path dissolves beneath her feet."


Theresa

Medium Humanoid (Sarkaz), Lawful Neutral

AC 24 Initiative +20 (30)
HP 170 (20d8+80)
Speed 40 ft.
ModSave ModSave ModSave
STR 16 +3 +3 DEX 17 +3 +3 CON 18 +4 +4
INT 20 +5 +13 WIS 18 +4 +12 CHA 26 +8 +8
Skills Perception +12, Medicine +12, History +13, Religion +13, Insight +16, Persuasion +16
Resistances Poison, Radiant, Necrotic
Immunities Psychic; Blinded, Deafened, Frightened, Charmed
Senses Blindsight 60 ft., Darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 22
Languages Common, Sarkaz, Victorian, Rim
CR 25 (75,000 XP; PB +8)

Traits

Boss Resistance (3/Day). When Theresa fails a saving throw, he can expend 50 HP to instead succeed. Additionally, Theresa can take Legendary Action and expend a use of Boss Resistance to regain 50 HP.

Magic Resistance. Theresa has Advantage on saving throws made to resist spells or other magical effects.

Civilight Eterna·Black-crowned Sage. If Theresis is within 120 ft. of Theresa and has more than 1 HP, Theresa is protected by a force and has Total Cover. Casting Dispel Magic at 9th level on Theresa suppresses this effect for 1 minute.

The Echo of Kings. Theresa adds her Charisma modifier to her AC (included above). Additionally, attack rolls made by all Sarkaz creatures against Theresa have Disadvantage.

The Past Lingering. Each time Theresa takes a Legendary Action, she and each ally within 20 ft. of her Emanation regain 10 HP.

The Myriad Dreams. 1 minute after Theresa rolls Initiative, if her HP is not reduced to 0, she immediately deals 105 (10d10+50) force damage that can't be reduced to each chosen creature within 1,200 ft. A creature reduced to 0 HP by this damage dies outright.


Actions

Multiattack. Theresa makes three Eternal Dust attacks, and she must choose a different target for each attack.

Eternal Dust. Melee Attack Roll: +16 to hit, reach 30 ft. Hit: 41 (6d10+8) radiant damage that can't be reduced. Miss: Half damage.

The Future Elusive. Constitution Saving Throw: DC 24 (has Advantage if within 10 ft. of Theresa), each enemy within Theresa's 30-ft. Emanation. Failure: 65 (10d12) radiant damage that can't be reduced, and the target is Restrained until the start of its turn. Success: Half damage only.

The Present Reconstructed (Recharge 6). Charisma Saving Throw: DC 24, each enemy within Theresa's 60-ft. Emanation. Failure: If the target is not Bloodied, its current HP is immediately set to half its maximum HP; otherwise, it takes 65 (10d12) radiant damage that can't be reduced.

Artcasting. Theresa casts one of the following spells as a 17th-level caster, using Charisma as her spellcasting ability (Spell Save DC 24, Spell Attack +16).

At will:Mage Hand, Prestidigitation, Dancing Lights, Minor Illusion, Message, Mending, Mage Armor (included in AC)

3/day each:Sanctuary, Blur, Slow, Dispel Magic, Dream ShearsDM2

2/day each:Sanctuary DecreeROH, Destructive RainsROH

1/day each:Disintegrate, Misfortune StormROH (7th-level version), Mind Blank (cast before combat), Imprisonment


Bonus Actions

Cage Spread. Theresa chooses all Cocoons on the battlefield, and summons additional Cocoons in as many ground spaces as possible within a 15-ft. cube area originating from each Cocoon.


Reactions

Convergence of Hope. Trigger: A creature within 120 ft. of Theresa that can hear her misses with an attack roll. Response: Theresa can choose to lose 20 HP, then change that attack to a hit.

Dust Barrier. Trigger: Theresa is about to take damage, and there are no enemies within 10 ft. of Theresa's Emanation. Response: Until the end of this turn, Theresa has the d12 Sanctuary condition. If at the end of the turn there are still no enemies in the area, Theresa regains the spent Reaction. If there are, those enemies take 20 radiant damage that can't be reduced.


Legendary Actions

Legendary Actions: 3 (4 in lair). Theresa can take one of the following actions immediately after another creature's turn. She regains all Legendary Actions at the start of her turn.

Swift Artcasting. Theresa uses her Cast a Spell action to cast a spell of 6th level or lower with a casting time of 1 action. She can't do so again until the start of her next turn.

Weave Cocoon. Dexterity Saving Throw: DC 24, one ground creature Theresa chooses within 120 ft. Success: The creature can immediately move 5 ft. to avoid being trapped by the Cocoon. Failure or Success: A Cocoon (AC 10, HP 30, Tiny object, disappears after 1 minute) appears beneath the creature's feet. While on the Cocoon, the creature is Stunned and Restrained until it leaves. If the creature destroys the Cocoon with an attack or direct effect, it is also affected by this.

Crown's Dread. Theresa chooses each creature within 120 ft. that is aware of her presence, releasing the crown's power to influence their minds. Wisdom Saving Throw: DC 24 (has Disadvantage if the target is a Sarkaz), each chosen creature. Failure: The target is Charmed or Frightened (chosen by Theresa), and retries the saving throw at the end of its turn, ending the effect on a success. The save automatically succeeds after 1 minute, and a creature that succeeds on the save is immune to this effect for 1 minute.



Multi-BOSS Combat

In a combat where multiple creatures have Legendary Actions, only the creature with the highest CR can take Legendary Actions. This means Theresa only uses the Legendary Actions above when fighting alone.
