Rhodes Operation Handbook：[莫菲丝]
Morpheus

Melee, Ranged, Art; Humanoid Leader (Perro)

Morpheus. The Dreamweaver who doesn't believe in fairy tales. Using her spinning wheel and weaving knife, she manipulates the battlefield with blue and red threads, dragging enemies into endless nightmares.


Eternal Slumber. She doesn't believe in the Dream Castle's original intention of "granting sweet dreams then letting go." She believes only by trapping people forever in dreams can she gain eternal companionship.


    "Everything in dreams is so interesting. Why would you want to escape... It's so nice to stay here."


Morpheus

Medium Humanoid (Perro), Chaotic Neutral

AC 17 (Mage Armor) Initiative +16 (26)
HP 300 (40d8+120)
Speed 35 ft.
ModSave ModSave ModSave
STR 9 -1 -1 DEX 18 +4 +4 CON 16 +3 +3
INT 14 +2 +2 WIS 15 +2 +2 CHA 22 +6 +6
Skills Arcana +8, Perception +8, Insight +8, Performance +12
Resistances Psychic
Immunities Poisoned, Frightened
Senses Darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 17
Languages Common, Victorian, Sylvan
CR 20 (25,000 XP; PB +6)

Traits

Dreamcast Resistance. When Morpheus fails a saving throw, she can actively sever one valid Blue Thread, then change the result to a success.

Dream Weaver. Morpheus can weave Soul-Binding Threads, summoning Blue Threads from her Spinning Wheel and Red Threads from her Weaving Knife. See the "Legendary Actions" section for thread effects. Soul-Binding Threads are Tiny objects with AC 20 and 20 HP, Immune to slashing, piercing, and bludgeoning damage, and they regain all HP at the start of Morpheus's turn. However, if other creatures are in the thread's path, the thread's effects and benefits for Morpheus are nullified. Finally, a connected target can escape and destroy a thread by succeeding on a DC 20 Strength saving throw at the end of each of its turns.

Echo of Lingering Dreams (Mythic Trait; Recharges on Long Rest). When Morpheus's HP is reduced to 0, she doesn't die and immediately deals 33 (6d10) psychic damage to each chosen creature within 120 ft. At the start of her next turn, her HP resets to 360, and she chooses each Dream Construct within 120 ft., connecting to them with Blue Threads. Additionally, for one hour, Morpheus can use the options in the "Mystic Actions" section.

Fey Ancestry. Morpheus has Advantage on saving throws made to resist Charmed, and magic can't put Morpheus to sleep.


Actions

Multiattack. Morpheus makes three attacks using either Weaving Knife or Spinning Wheel.

Weaving Knife. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit (has Advantage when attacking Red Thread targets), reach 5 ft. Hit: 21 (5d6+4) slashing damage. For each active thread, this attack deals an additional 3 (1d6) psychic damage.

Spinning Wheel. Ranged Weapon Attack: +12 to hit (has Advantage when attacking Red Thread targets and unlimited range), range 120 ft. Hit: 22 (3d10+6) psychic damage. For each active thread, this attack deals an additional 5 (1d10) psychic damage.

Artcasting. Morpheus casts one of the following spells as a 13th-level caster, requiring no material components and using Charisma as her spellcasting ability (Spell Save DC 20, Spell Attack +12).

At will:Mind Sliver, Prestidigitation, Dancing Lights, Guidance, Minor Illusion, Mage Armor (cast before combat)

3/day each:Silent Image, Mirror Image, Major Image, Dispel Magic

2/day each:Phantasmal Force, Dream

1/day each:Dominate Person, Synaptic Static


Bonus Actions

Retention of Consciousness (2/Day). Morpheus summons a number of Dream Construct creatures with a combined HP not exceeding 200 in visible open spaces within 120 ft.


Reactions

Dream Evolution (2/Day). Trigger: Morpheus becomes Bloodied by a source of damage. Response: Morpheus immediately gains 100 HP of Temporary HP. While this Temporary HP persists, her speed increases to 60 ft., and she is Immune to Grappled or Restrained. If her Echo of Lingering Dreams trait is activated, she also gains a flying speed of 60 ft. (hover).


Legendary Actions

Legendary Actions: 3 (4 in lair). Morpheus can take one of the following actions immediately after another creature's turn. She regains all Legendary Actions at the start of her turn.

Dream Entanglement. Morpheus chooses two enemies within 60 ft. that aren't connected, and connects to them with Red Threads, lasting until one of them is reduced to 0 HP. While the threads are connected and active, each time Morpheus uses this action again, the targets connected by Red Threads take 22 (4d10) psychic damage.

Sensory Deprivation. Morpheus chooses two allies within 60 ft. that aren't connected, and connects to them with Blue Threads, lasting until one of them is reduced to 0 HP. While the threads are connected and active, each time Morpheus takes damage, she can transfer the damage she takes and distribute it to the targets connected by active Blue Threads, but each target can take no more than 10 damage from this at one time.

Swift Artcasting. Morpheus uses her Cast a Spell action to cast a spell of 3rd level or lower with a casting time of 1 action. She can't do so again until the start of her next turn.


Mystic Actions

If Morpheus's Echo of Lingering Dreams trait is activated, she can choose from the following options as Legendary Actions.

Shackles of the Silent Dream. Wisdom Saving Throw: DC 20, one chosen creature connected by an active Red Thread. Failure: 44 (8d10) psychic damage, and the target is Charmed until the start of Morpheus's next turn. Success: Half damage only. A creature reduced to 0 HP by this damage is lifted by the thread, becoming Unconscious and Restrained until it regains HP to full or is affected by a Greater Restoration spell.

Braided Silk Threads. Morpheus uses either Dream Entanglement or Sensory Deprivation, but the maximum distance increases to 300 ft.

Dreamland Wandering. Morpheus moves up to half her speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks, then uses Weaving Knife or Spinning Wheel to make one attack.
