Preface

Welcome to the Rhodes Operations Handbook | ROH (5E2024). This expansion rulebook aims to combine the world of a certain anime tower defense game with the core rules of the world's greatest roleplaying game, 5th Edition, to bring players a brand-new adventure experience. Whether you are a die-hard fan of the original work or a TRPG player unfamiliar with it, this book will provide you with rich content and streamlined rules, allowing you to easily create characters and dive into combat.

In the Rhodes Operations Handbook (hereafter referred to as ROH), we have attempted to simplify many of the complex rules commonly found in other Arknights expansion books while faithfully preserving the core elements of the original work. The goal of ROH is to allow every player, regardless of familiarity with the original game, to quickly pick it up and enjoy the fun.

This book not only provides detailed character options, subclasses, races, feats, and spells but also includes a bestiary and campaign-building advice, allowing DMs to use a wealth of enemy resources and world-building materials to craft their own Terra campaigns and modules.
